- Attached garages can often contribute to poor indoor air-quality (oil, gas, paint, chemicals, engine-exhaust). All surfaces (walls, floors) betwen the garage
and the home should be **especially** well sealed to ensure against any contaminants entering the home. The [US Dept. of Energy provides additional resources and research](https://basc.pnnl.gov/resource-guides/air-sealing-attached-garage) 
related to sealing off attached garages.
- Primary airtightness layer to be [Pro-Clima Solitex 1000](https://475.supply/products/solitex-mento-1000) or sim. vapor-permeable
self-adhered WRB membrane applied along the 'outer' (Garage side) stud wall.
- For all stud-cavity batt insulation, recommended to use a formaldehyde-free product in order to ensure healthy indoor air and
environmental quality. [Thermafiber](https://www.owenscorning.com/en-us/insulation/commercial/formaldehyde-free) or sim.
