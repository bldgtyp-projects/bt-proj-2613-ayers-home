Note: All concrete sizing as per Structural Engineer. Concrete is shown for illustration purposes only.

- Install 2” sub-slab [Rockwool Comfortboard 80](https://www.rockwool.com/north-america/products/comfortboard/).
- Sub-slab air and vapor barrier to be installed directly below concrete (above insulation). 
Use 1[5 Mil StegoHome](https://475.supply/products/stegohome-15-mil-vapor-barrier) or similar
- Tape / Seal all penetrations and seams of sub-slab air and vapor barrier with 
[StegoTape](https://475.supply/products/stego-tape) / [StegoMastic](https://475.supply/products/stego-mastic) or similar.
