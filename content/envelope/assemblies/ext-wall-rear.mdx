- Primary airtightness layer to be [Pro-Clima Visconn](https://475.supply/products/visconn-blueblack) or sim. liquid
  applied airseal applied to inside of face of new CMU wall.
- [3-1/2" SmartRock](https://475.supply/products/smartrock) mineral-fiber + Intello installed inside of new CMU masonry
  wall.
- 2x service cavity stud wall installed inside of SmartRock insulation layer.
- Use 'Service Cavity' on interior side for all plumbing and electrical. Reduce penetrations through the air-barrier to
  improve building airtightness.
