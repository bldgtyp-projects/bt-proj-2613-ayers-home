- Primary airtightness layer to be [Pro-Clima Adhero 3000](https://475.supply/products/adhero-3000) or sim. vapor-permeable
self-adhered WRB membrane applied to the outer face of the plywood sheathing.
- [7" Rockwool Comfortboard-80](https://www.rockwool.com/north-america/products/comfortboard/) mineral-fiber installed as continuous
exterior insulation. 
- For all rafter-cavity batt insulation, recommended to use a formaldehyde-free product in order to ensure healthy indoor air and
environmental quality. [Thermafiber](https://www.owenscorning.com/en-us/insulation/commercial/formaldehyde-free) or sim.
- Note: when constructing un-vented insulated rafter assemblies follow all code requirements as per [Residential Code of
  RI 2021 section 806.5](https://up.codes/viewer/rhode_island/irc-2021/chapter/8/roof-ceiling-construction#R806.5)
- In Zone 5, when constructing the roof without venting, min R-20 insulation should be used **above/outside** the primary sheathing
  layer. R-20 can be achieved by:
  - 5" of [Rockwool Comfortboard-80](https://www.rockwool.com/north-america/products/comfortboard/)
  - 5" EPS foam board
  - 4" of GPS foam board
  - 4" of XPS foam board
  - 4" of Polyisocyanurate foam board.
