- Primary airtightness layer to be [Pro-Clima Adhero 3000](https://475.supply/products/adhero-3000) or sim. vapor-permeable
self-adhered WRB membrane applied to the outer face of the plywood sheathing (behind the continuous insulation). 
- [4" Rockwool Comfortboard-80](https://www.rockwool.com/north-america/products/comfortboard/) mineral-fiber installed as continuous
exterior insulation. 
- Support cladding by using either wood battens fastened with stainless-steel screws, or low-conductivity facade clips. See the
[Rockwool Cladding Attachment Guide](https://www.rockwool.com/siteassets/o2-rockwool/documentation/technical-guides/commercial/cavityrock-and-comfortboard-cladding-attachment-solutions-technical-guide.pdf) 
for more details.
- For all stud-cavity batt insulation, recommended to use a formaldehyde-free product in order to ensure healthy indoor air and
environmental quality. [Thermafiber](https://www.owenscorning.com/en-us/insulation/commercial/formaldehyde-free) or sim.
