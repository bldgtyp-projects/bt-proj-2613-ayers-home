The **Phius REVIVE** protocol suggests that during the simulation test-period the [Heat Index](https://bigladdersoftware.com/epx/docs/22-2/engineering-reference/resilience-metrics.html#heat-index) temperature should be 
used to evaluate the overall perceived thermal habitability of the zone. In the results shown below, the Heat Index 
for each zone is plotted for the entire test-period summer week. Minimum compliance with the **Phius REVIVE** standard 
requires that the building demonstrate:

- 0-hours of **Heat Index** in the ‘Extreme Danger’ zone ( > 52 °C [125 °F]).
