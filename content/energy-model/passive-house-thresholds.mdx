In order to evaluate a building’s performance, [total annual energy consumption](#site-energy) is key, as shown above. 
However, in addition to this top line figure the ‘Passive House’ framework suggests that the building 
should also meet additional heating and cooling annual energy demand performance targets. It is also 
useful to compare the peak-heating and peak-cooling loads to the recommended limits for Passive House 
buildings. While these limits are not required for certification in all cases, it is still good practice 
to attempt to meet them where possible. Where the home fails to meet these targets is a clear indication 
that improvements are possible.
