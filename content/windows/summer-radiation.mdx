- Even taking into account the site's tree cover, reasonably high solar radiation is observed along the 
south facade which will lead to reduce thermal comfort for the occupants.
- It is recommended to prioritize shading for the south windows. Use robust user-operable interior blinds
with high opacity and solar reflectance. Specify glazing with low SHGC (g-Value) for all south facing glazing. 
- Where feasible and in alignment with the design, use tree plantings, trellis, shades, shutters, or other elements to 
provide additional shading for all south-facing and all west-facing glazing. 
- **Note:** it is assumed that the west-side has significant tree cover which provides useful shaded against late-afternoon
summer sun. If west-side trees are removed as part of construction, this will affect thermal comfort in the space.
