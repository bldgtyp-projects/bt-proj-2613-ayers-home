The energy balance of the windows is critical on any high-performance building project. The goal for this climate should
be to take some advantage of wintertime solar gains in order to reduce overall heating energy need, while being
extremely cautious about the potential for overheating and increasing cooling energy need. This can be particularly
challenging with highly glazed rooms or space which feature large amounts of south or west facing glass.

satellite images from google maps and plot lines from [OpenStreet
